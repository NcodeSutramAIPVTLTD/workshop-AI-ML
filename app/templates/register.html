Registration Details

SHIVA 2024 (fully online mode) is an internship program sponsored under the SPOKE program of IIT BHU and IIIT Allahabad, for learning the concepts of machine learning and deep learning.

Fill the registration form and subsequently pay the registration fee to get registered.

Registered candidates can login where they can pay the course fee to avail online resources such as link for the online classes, recorded videos, and other such e-materials.

Registration deadline:-16-July-2024

Course Start:-17-July-2024

Course Duration:-17-July-2024 to 7 August (3 week)

Registration Fee:-Rs. 2000.00

Course Fee:-Rs. 4700.00
